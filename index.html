About the Hackathon

Join us for HackathonX, a 36-hour event where teams of up to 4 participants will collaborate on innovative projects. We welcome developers, designers, students, and anyone passionate about technology and problem-solving. This year's theme is "Tech for Good: Building Solutions for a Sustainable Future."

We'll provide expert mentors, hands-on workshops, and all the resources you need to transform your ideas into reality. Compete for exciting prizes awarded to the most creative, impactful, and technically impressive projects. Register now and be a part of something amazing!
