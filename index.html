About HackX

HackX is a premier individual coding competition where passionate developers come together to solve real-world problems through technology. This 36-hour event challenges you to push your limits and create innovative solutions.

This year's theme is "Tech for Good: Building Solutions for a Sustainable Future." We'll provide expert mentors, hands-on workshops, and all the resources you need to transform your ideas into reality. Compete for prizes worth ₹1,00,000 awarded to the most creative, impactful, and technically impressive projects.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can't find your answer here? Contact us directly!

Who can participate in HackX?

HackX is open to all individual participants aged 18 and above. Whether you're a student, professional, or hobbyist, we welcome all skill levels. This is an individual competition - no teams allowed!

What should I prepare for the event?

You'll need your laptop, charger, and any specific software/tools you plan to use. We recommend preparing your development environment in advance. For in-person participants, we'll provide workspace, food, and drinks.

Can I participate remotely?

Absolutely! HackX offers both in-person and remote participation options. Remote participants will have full access to all workshops, mentorship, and judging via our online platform.

How are projects judged?

Projects are evaluated on innovation, technical complexity, design, and potential impact. Judges will assess how well your solution addresses the problem and the quality of your implementation.

What technologies can I use?

You can use any programming languages, frameworks, or tools you're comfortable with. The choice is yours! We only require that your submission is original work created during the hackathon.
